⚠️ Emergency Procedure
  1. Call 000 for an ambulance (start CPR if needed).
  2. Call the Coordinator (Care Partner) or office so the participant's emergency contact can be notified.
  3. Stay with the participant and reassure them until help arrives.
  4. Provide the ambulance with medical information (contact Coordinator/Care Partner if unsure).
  5. Whether taken to hospital or not, notify the Coordinator (Care Partner) or office immediately.
  6. Complete and email the incident form to the Coordinator (Care Partner).
